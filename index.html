About Belgium

Belgium is a country located in Western Europe known for its medieval towns, Renaissance architecture, and as the headquarters of the European Union and NATO. Discover the heart of Europe with its rich culture, delicious cuisine, and stunning landscapes.

Why Visit Belgium?

  • 🍫 Delicious Belgian chocolates
  • 🧇 World-famous waffles
  • 📚 Rich history and culture
  • 🏛️ Beautiful architecture
  • 🏙️ Vibrant cities like Brussels, Bruges, and Antwerp

Fun Facts About Belgium

  • 🎺 The saxophone was invented by Belgian Adolphe Sax in 1846
  • 🍟 French fries actually originated in Belgium, not France!
  • 💎 Antwerp is the diamond capital of the world
  • 🗣️ Belgium has three official languages: Dutch, French, and German
