History of Belgium

Belgium has a rich history that dates back to the Roman Empire. It has been influenced by various cultures and has played a significant role in European history.

Key Historical Events

  • Roman conquest and settlement
  • Formation of the Burgundian Netherlands
  • Independence from Spain in 1830
  • World War I and II impacts
  • Establishment of the European Union headquarters
